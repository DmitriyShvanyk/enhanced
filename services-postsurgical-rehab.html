postsurgical rehab

After surgery, what both you and your doctor want most is a rapid, successful recovery. Enhanced PT provides advanced, hands-on postsurgical care customized to your individual needs. With biomechanical evaluation and muscle strengthening/conditioning to help prevent future injuries we provide "Enhanced" rehab services that can get you active again, quickly and safely.
This includes post surgical rehabilitation programs following surgeries such as...

Rotator Cuff Repairs (Open or Arthroscopic)
Shoulder Impingements & Labral Tears
ACL, MCL, LCL, PCL, Meniscus Reconstructions
Total Joint Replacements (Shoulder, Hip & Knee)
Carpal Tunnel Release
Tennis Elbow Surgery

For Physicians

To give your patients the best possible rehab results, we'll assess their needs as well as the unique aspects of their condition. We'll develop a rehab program based on their specific condition with a goal to work as a team and create a rehab program that will help our patients get back to their active lifestyle safely and with least amount of time.
