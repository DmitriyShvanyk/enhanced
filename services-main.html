Enhanced Physical Therapy

Enhanced Physical Therapy is a physical therapy practice, where our aim is to restore a person’s best ability to function through minimizing physical impairment, functional limitations, and disability-related congenital and acquired conditions. At Enhanced PT we treat people from all age groups, at any point during their life. Our aim is to promote wellness, healthy lifestyles, and education about ways to prevent injuries and loss of movement. The highlights of the program are as follows:

1. Your program begins with an examination consisting of cutting edge evaluation procedures, including Cyriax and Maitland guidelines, to determine the kind of treatment regimen ideal for you.

2. Your individualized program will be designed using physical therapy modalities such as electric stimulation, cold laser and ultrasound using the latest and the most efficient devices that are currently available. Mobilizations of the spine or peripheral joints are performed using Mulligan, Maitland, Kaltenborn, and McKenzie techniques. Manual traction, Myofasical release and evidence-based therapeutic exercises, nuero-muscular reeducation, gait, and balance training are all incorporated into your program for optimizing recovery.

3. These modalities will be used to relieve pain, reduce swelling, restore joint, muscle and nerve function in the affected areas. Along with all of the above mentioned, re-education of proper posture and biomechanics is essential for optimal alignment and injury prevention.

Services We Offer

What is direct access?

In New York State, you can receive treatment from a licensed Physical Therapist for your condition without the need of receiving a referral or prescription from your doctor.

The law, Chapter 298 of the Laws of 2006, allows physical therapists with three years of practical experience to treat patients without a referral beginning November 23, 2006.

What does this mean for you?

  • You can receive treatment quicker by not waiting to receive a referral from your doctor
  • Saves you money by reducing you trips to the doctor
  • If we believe further intervention is required, we can provide you with doctors who can handle your case best

does my insurance allow for direct access?

We verify your insurance benefits before your first visit. If your insurance company does not approve of Direct Access, we can refer you to one of our Physicians to handle your care and provide you with a referral. Direct access treatment without a referral is limited to 10 visits or 30 days, whichever occurs first. A referral or prescription is still required for all patients that have coverage with Medicare, Workers’ Compensation, or No Fault.

Wellness program

The Wellness program is exclusively for current patients of Enhanced Physical Therapy that have exhausted all insurance benefits and wish to continue supervised therapeutic exercise. Patients pay out-of-pocket for the Wellness Program on a monthly basis. Candidates for Wellness have reached most of their physical therapy goals and are looking to maintain the strength, flexibility, and functional mobility gained with the providers they trust at Enhanced Physical Therapy.
