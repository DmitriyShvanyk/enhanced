SPORTS PHYSICAL THERAPY

We now offer Sports/Medical Massage!

As a patient at Enhanced PT you can be confident that you will receive the most up to date and excellent quality care. You will learn and be trained in programs to return to your sport or activity with guaranteed better performance without a risk of re-injury. We utilize evidence based programs and techniques to get back patient from post injury to sports specific phase and prepare them for performance.

We are dedicated to delivering the finest sports rehab care possible. Over the years, our hands-on care, premier strength and conditioning and sports specific protocols has helped many of people from all walks of life get and stay active.

Includes:

Shoulder Impingement
Rotator Cuff Injuries
Tendinitis
Meniscal / Cartilage Injuries
ACL Reconstruction
Plantar Fasciitis
Golfer's / Tennis Elbow
Muscle Strains
ITB Syndrome
