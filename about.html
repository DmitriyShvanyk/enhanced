About

Compassion Drives
Our Passion for Excellence
"Excellent Care starts with Compassion"

Enhanced Physical Therapy is a physical therapy practice that consistently uses current research and knowledge, while understanding personal limits, in treatment of patients. We integrate judgment and the patient/client perspective, embrace advancement, challenge mediocrity, and work toward development of new knowledge and patient satisfaction.

At Enhanced Physical Therapy the primary regard is towards the interest of each individual patient, thus assuming the responsibility of giving precedence to the needs of the patient.

Commitment to the community: We commit to meeting our obligations to provide effective physical therapy services to individual patients, to serve the profession, and to positively influence the health and wellness of the community.

Excellence: At Enhanced Physical Therapy we possess steadfast devotion to high ethical principles and professional standards.
